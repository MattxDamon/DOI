WARNING

Although Dawn of Industry is free to play, there are in-game purchases that will tempt the player to purchase virtual items. Items that players may purhcase include:

  • Decorative houshold items- These items impress politicians, giving the player political influence to support laws or taxes.
  • Company truck customization- Truck customization increases brand awareness of the paying player in the minds of both other human players, as well as virtual consumers.

Disclaimer

The legal and political scenarios in Dawn of Industry aren't meant to simulate or represent historical US law. Particular instances of innaccuracy are listed below:

  • The initial political resistance for merging two companies isn't a process that exists or has ever existed under US law.
  • Legal procedings limiting the number of investments in specific industries aren't historically accurate, these limitations exist to preserve fair gameplay.
